Modern Villa

This modern villa project explores the relationship between indoor and outdoor spaces, emphasizing natural light, open layouts, and minimal material palettes.

The design prioritizes clean lines and functional spaces, ensuring a seamless flow between living areas while maintaining privacy and comfort.

Sustainable materials and passive design strategies were integrated to reduce energy consumption and enhance environmental performance.
